> just for Gecode (added to the system environment variable Path)
> The compilation of GecodeJ by mean of ant, builds a jar. But no dll. How can

The ant build-file is just for compiling the Java files. For compiling
Gecode/J you should use the Makefile instead. It will in turn also
invoke ant to compile the Java-files.
