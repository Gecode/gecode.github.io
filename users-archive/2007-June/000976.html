Hi.

Nick Hindle wrote:
> In the All-Interval series example, I cannot work out how values  
> for the private member IntVarArray x are initialised.
>
> According to the specification in www.CSPLib.org, this var array  
> holds the numbers 0,1,...11 to represent the pitch-classes c, c#,  
> d, etc.
>
> Could someone help me understand how they are initialised in this  
> example?

This is the more general version, also explained in prob007 of  
csplib. Instead of a sequence x_0,...,x_11 with domains 0..11, you  
have a sequence x_0,...,x_n with domains 0..n. Initialization of x is  
done using the initializer expression x(this, opt.size, 0, opt.size -  
1).
