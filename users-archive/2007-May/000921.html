Hi,

Distinct, alldistinct, alldifferent, and alldiff are all common
synonyms for the constraint that all variables must take
distinct/different values.

In some solvers, the different names are used to refer to different
amounts of propagation. The corresponding concept in Gecode is
specifying naive, bounds, or domain consistent with the IntConLevel
argument.
