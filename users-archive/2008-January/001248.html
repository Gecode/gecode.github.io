Use Gecode::rel instead:

http://www.gecode.org/gecode-doc-latest/group__TaskModelIntCard.html

> Gecode::distinct
>
> I got this error:
>
>  gecol131/glue.cpp:1428: error: no matching function for call to  
> 'distinct(Gecode::Space*&, Gecode::SetVarArgs&, unsigned int&)'

decomposition instead.
