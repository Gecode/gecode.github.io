________________________ Submission is open _________________________

                               CP 2008

                     14th International Conference
                                  on
           Principles and Practice of Constraint Programming

      Sydney,  Australia                     14-18 September 2008

                 Co-located with: ICAPS, KR, and NMR.


   The CP conference is the annual international conference on
   constraint programming.  Papers are solicited from all disciplines
   concerned with constraints.  Reports on successful applications of
   constraint technology are particularly encouraged.  The conference
   proceedings will be published in the Springer LNCS series.

   Research papers can be Long (15 pages), or Short (2-5 pages)
   presenting preliminary work.


   Submissions are due on 1 April 2008. The submission server is open.


   Further information is provided on the conference webpage:

       http://www.csse.unimelb.edu.au/cp2008/
