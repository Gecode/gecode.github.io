_________________ Final Call for Workshop Proposals _________________

                                CP 2008

                     14th International Conference
                                  on
           Principles and Practice of Constraint Programming

      Sydney,  Australia                     14-18 September 2008

                 http://www.csse.unimelb.edu.au/cp2008/

                  Co-located with: ICAPS, KR, and NMR.


   The CP 2008 Program Committee invites proposals for the Workshop
   Program.  The topics of the workshops can cover any area related to
   constraints and any related cross-disciplinary areas.  Workshops
   related to planning and scheduling are especially encouraged
   because of the co-location with ICAPS 2008.  Further information is
   available in the full call for proposals:

     http://www.csse.unimelb.edu.au/cp2008/call-for-workshops.html

   Note that proposals are due in one week.  The deadlines are:

     Proposal submission                 31 March     2008
     Acceptance notification             16 April     2008
     Workshop CfP and web page            1 May       2008
     Publication of accepted papers      16 July      2008
     Workshop working notes              23 August    2008
     Workshops                        14-15 September 2008


   All proposals should be submitted to the Workshop Chair:

     Jimmy Lee
     The Chinese University of Hong Kong
     jlee [at] cse.cuhk.edu.hk
