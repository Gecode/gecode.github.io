Are you sure you provide an implementation for:

Gecode::Aminadistinct(Gecode::Space&,  
Gecode::VarArgArray<Gecode::IntVar> const&, Gecode::IntConLevel)


The problem can also be the way you link your executable. I think the  
problem is that you didn't provide an implementation for it or you  
forgot to link the object file of the propagator when linking your  
example.

you can try:

$g++ -I/usr/local/include/ -c Aminadistinct.cpp -o Aminadistinct.o
$g++ -I/usr/local/include/ -c example.cpp
$g++ -L/usr/local/lib/ example Aminadistinct.o -lgecodeint - 
lgecodesearch
