> I know that problem: unfortunately the windows headers you include define
> the macros min and max (which is a bug in the windows headers). What you
> should do is after including the windows headers files, add the following:
>
>
>
> #ifdef min
>
> #undef min
>
> #endif
>
>
>
> #ifdef max
>
> #undef max
>
> #endif
>
>
>
> As you don’t tell us which version of Gecode you use (one should ALWAYS do
> that) I can only suggest to have a look at gecode/support/thread.hh in the
> latest version of Gecode (3.1.0). There at the very end you’ll find that
> fix.
>
>
>
