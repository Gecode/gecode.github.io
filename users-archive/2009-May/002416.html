causing compilation to fail for me (unknown sleep(3) and usleep(3)) in
both 3.1.0 and trunk:

#ifdef GECODE_HAS_UNISTD_H
#include <unistd.h>
#endif

or perhaps more correctly:

#ifdef GECODE_HAS_UNISTD_H
extern "C" {
#include <unistd.h>
}
#endif
