Well what do you want to do?  The following works for example:
      IntVarArgs D(*this, n*m, 0,1);
      Matrix<IntVarArgs> m(D,n,m);
The following works, too:
      IntArgs D(n*m);
      Matrix<IntArgs> m(D,n,m);

Guido
